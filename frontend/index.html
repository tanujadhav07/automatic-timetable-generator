System Guidelines

  • 1
    Always list subjects exactly as they appear in the teacher's expertise list.
  • 2
    Maintain at least a 1:5 Teacher-to-Lecture ratio for the best scheduling results.
  • 3
    The 12:00 - 1:00 PM slot is globally reserved for the Lunch Break.

Teacher Workload Guide

  • Blue highlighted slots on the teacher schedule indicate active teaching duties.
  • "Free" slots are reserved for preparation, grading, or administrative work.
  • The PDF export automatically creates a clean landscape layout for faculty members.

Zero Conflict Policy

Our generator utilizes a constraint-satisfaction heuristic that prevents a single teacher from being scheduled in two places at once, across any class in the system.
